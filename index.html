WHO WE ARE

GTS is a specialised live sport broadcast production service. It offers integrated solution of quality broadcast production, multi-angle live footage, audio and graphics integration and audiovisual distribution to broadcast channels. GTS has been serving multiple live event production since its establishment and will continue to work together with rightsholders in delivering exciting and glorious moments in live sports and entertainment events to the avid fans watching from various devices.

History

Definition and Story

2017

  • After PSSI sanction is lifted and official league operator is established
  • GTS continued to serve the professional footbal sector through providing a quality live sport production services
  • GTS is among the first broadcast production company to introduce more than 8 angle camera footage in one football match production
  • Producing HD quality broadcast to provide a more enticing football game moments to the audience
  • The official live production partner status is continued until the present day

2016

  • GTS was first established in 2016 as football league operator during the football ‘vacuum’ period to fill in the competition gap due to FIFA sanction for PSSI (Football Association of Indonesia)
  • As operator, GTS managed Indonesia Soccer Championship that was participated by 18 clubs at the time
  • During this period, GTS experienced the complexity of football league operation first-hand and learned early for live sport production process.
